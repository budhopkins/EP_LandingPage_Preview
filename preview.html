Terms and Conditions

These terms apply to eligible EveryPlate customers who receive a direct SMS invitation to submit a cancellation request for an upcoming order after cut off. By submitting a request through this page, the customer acknowledges that EveryPlate may accept or decline the request having regard to the status of the relevant order. If EveryPlate accepts the request, account credit may be applied as follows: 90% account credit where the request is submitted within 15 hours after cut off, 60% account credit where the request is submitted more than 15 hours and up to 48 hours after cut off, and 0% account credit where the request is submitted more than 48 hours after cut off. Any account credit offered under this process will be applied to the customer's EveryPlate account within 48 hours of the cancellation being completed and will not be refunded to the original payment method.

This process is available only to customers identified by EveryPlate as eligible for this activity and who receive access to this page through the relevant SMS communication. Submission of a request does not guarantee cancellation or account credit. Personal information collected through this page will be handled in accordance with EveryPlate's Privacy Policy for the purpose of assessing and processing the request.
